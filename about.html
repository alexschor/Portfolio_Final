ALEXANDRA SCHORNDORF

Alexandra Schorndorf is a New York City-based illustrator and designer who spends her weekdays working for the Tasting Table. She illustrates top chefs, dynamic recipes, creates interactive web graphics, and develops packaging design for lotions and soaps. Alexandra has recently expanded her skill-set by adding UX/UI design, along with web development skills, to her repertoire.

During the evenings and weekends she curates her lifestyle blog, Bon Vivant, with her most recent illustrations, travel guides, recipes, and vivid inspiration.

Alexandra holds a BFA degree in Illustration and Art History from the Fashion Institue of Technology. She has also studied classical painting and drawing at Polimoda in Florence, Italy.

She loves to work with clients to create thoughtful and beautiful design solutions; working in the realms of illustration, web/mobile, and graphic design. For inquires and freelance opportunities, please feel free to reach out!
